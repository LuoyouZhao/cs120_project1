Album Introduction

"Life is Hot Because of You" is an album by New Pants released on April 7, 2016. It features 10 tracks and is known for capturing urban stories and sentiments. The album includes songs that express concern for city dwellers and their situations, with emotional and sensitive undertones that contrast with the band's typically cool stage presence.
